Services

At DentCare, we care for the health and beauty of your smile with professional services and modern treatments.

Dental Check-up and Consultation

Professional visit to diagnose the health of teeth and gums, identify problems, and create a personalized treatment plan according to your needs.

Teeth Cleaning and Whitening

Removal of bacterial plaque and tartar to prevent dental diseases. Offering whitening treatments for a brighter and more radiant smile.

Dental Fillings

Repair of teeth damaged by cavities with quality materials that match the natural color of the teeth, restoring functionality and aesthetics.

Dental Implants

Replacement of lost teeth with titanium implants, which ensure maximum stability, full functionality, and a long-lasting natural appearance.

Orthodontics (Dental Braces)

Correction of teeth position and bite alignment with fixed or transparent braces, for a healthy and aesthetic smile.

Oral Surgery and Tooth Extraction

Extraction of damaged or irreparable teeth and specialized oral surgical interventions for advanced and safe treatments.
